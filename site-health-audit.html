Why this exists

Most audit tools score one pillar. AI search is happening on three.

SEO is still required. It is not enough on its own anymore.

Pillar 01

SEO

How Google ranks your site in traditional search. Titles, schema, canonicals, sitemaps, page speed. Most audit tools stop here and call it a day.

Pillar 02

AEO

How your site reads to voice assistants, featured snippets, and Google's AI overviews. Different rules. Different signals. Mostly invisible to a regular SEO audit.

Pillar 03

GEO

How ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini decide whether to cite you when someone asks a question your business could answer. New game. Most agencies are not even tracking it yet.

What this means in plain English. Your competitors are showing up inside ChatGPT answers and you are not. Or you are, and you do not know why some pages get cited and others get skipped. The Site Health Audit scores all three pillars in one pass so you can see the whole picture.
