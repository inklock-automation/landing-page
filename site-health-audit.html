Most audit tools score one thing. AI search is happening on three fronts now.

SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

How Google ranks your site in traditional search results. Most audit tools stop here. Most of them give you a number and call it a day.

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)

How your site reads to voice assistants, featured snippets, and Google's AI overviews. Different rules. Different signals. Mostly invisible to regular SEO audits.

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

How ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini decide whether to cite you when someone asks them a question your business could answer. New game. Most agencies aren't even tracking it yet.

What this means in plain English: Your competition is showing up in ChatGPT answers and you're not. Or you are and you don't know why some pages get cited and others don't. We score all three pillars in the same audit so you can see the full picture.
