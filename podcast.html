Common questions about the InkLock podcast

What is the InkLock podcast about?

Real conversations about the unglamorous work behind growing a service business: operations, marketing systems, AI integration, and the things that go wrong before they go right.

How often does the podcast publish new episodes?

We publish on a bi-weekly cadence, with shorter episode drops in between when something newsworthy hits the small-business tooling world.

Who should listen to this podcast?

Founders and operators of service businesses who want practical takes on systems and automation, not motivational speakers and not pure tech specs.
